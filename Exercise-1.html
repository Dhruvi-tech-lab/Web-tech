About Us

Established in 2004, Government Engineering College, Gandhinagar (GEC-Gn) is a premier institution offering quality technical education. It aims to develop skilled engineers and professionals to contribute to society.

Courses Offered

  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.)
  • Master of Engineering (M.E.)
  • Diploma Courses
  • Research Programs (Ph.D.)

Departments

  • Computer Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Instrumentation & Control Engineering
  • Robotics and Automation
